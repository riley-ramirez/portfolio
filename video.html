How scientists are using a tiny tracking device to save grassland birds.

This was a video made for my final project in J296 Animating the News class at the UC Berkeley Journalism School, where we were tasked with pitching, shooting and editing an animated explainer video. This explainer was also part of a longer story I was covering for my master's thesis on the decline of grassland birds in North America. For the story, I traveled to New Mexico to document researchers monitoring and tagging grassland birds in the Chihuahuan Desert using a Canon C100 camera and DJI Pocket Osmo III. I used Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, Illustrator and Procreate to edit, illustrate and animate the video.
